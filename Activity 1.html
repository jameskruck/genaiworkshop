Activity Introduction

Welcome to the Activity

Overview

In this activity, you will explore how AI can support collaborative problem-solving and creative thinking. The activity consists of two parts:

  • Part 1: Create a Choose Your Own Adventure (CYOA) story, using AI to refine your ideas and explore new possibilities.
  • Part 2: Simulate a student experience by completing a real-world assignment with AI assistance and reflecting on how AI can influence assessment design.

Objectives

  • Explore how AI can act as a creative partner by refining ideas and generating new possibilities.
  • Engage in hands-on activities to simulate student use of AI for completing assignments.
  • Analyze how AI-generated responses can influence assessment design and inform authentic assessment practices.
